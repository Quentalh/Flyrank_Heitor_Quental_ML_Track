## My Three Target Tasks

**1. Udemy (Python course)**
* **What "Done Well" means:** Watching a minimum of 20 classes (7–10 minutes each) and actively understanding and applying the code — not passive viewing. This implies pausing to test snippets, taking brief notes, or re-running examples to confirm comprehension before moving to the next class.

**2. ISC2 (Cybersecurity certificate)**
* **What "Done Well" means:** Completing between 50% and 100% of the current module, with high retention — prioritizing depth over speed. If retention starts dropping, stop and consolidate rather than pushing further into the module just to hit a percentage.

**3. Flyrank**
* **What "Done Well" means:** Completing a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 5 assignments, done efficiently — meaning solid output without over-investing time per assignment. Use the dashboard review (Task 4) to select the highest-value assignments first, so effort is concentrated where it matters most.
